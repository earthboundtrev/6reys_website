# 6reys Arcade

A modern arcade website showcasing our gaming venue, events, and entertainment offerings. 


## About Our Site

- **Home** - Your gateway to 6reys Arcade, featuring our latest attractions and upcoming events.
- **About** - Learn about our arcade's history, mission, and the team behind the magic.
- **Menu** - Browse our selection of snacks, drinks, and refreshments available at our concession stand.
- **Games** - Explore our extensive collection of arcade games, from classic favorites to the latest releases.
- **Parties** - Discover our party packages and event hosting options for birthdays, corporate events, and special occasions.
- **Contact** - Find our location, operating hours, and ways to get in touch with our team.
